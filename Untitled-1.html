Come Find Me, When You're Ready

I don't think I can love someone else the way I love you. We may not communicate for days, and those days might eventually turn to months and years.

But know that in my heart there will always be a door waiting for you to come through. If the universe permits it, maybe we'd meet again as better people in better circumstances. If it happens, I hope I can treat you right and give what we had a second chance, and this time around we will do it right.

Though our story ended up tragically, I will always love you, and the memory of the broken promises and dreams of us will remain forever in my heart.

I will always miss you.

Thank you for letting me have a Chance of loving you.
